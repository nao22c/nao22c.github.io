Thank you for using Shareholder Benefit Log (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled within the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App stores the following information entered by the user, locally on your device only:

  • Stock ticker codes, company names, purchase dates, share quantities, and acquisition prices
  • Shareholder benefit details, expiration dates, and record dates

This data is stored only in your iPhone's local storage and is never sent to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The App communicates with the following third-party services:

  • Yahoo Finance API: Used to fetch stock prices, financial metrics, and shareholder benefit information. Only ticker codes are transmitted.
  • Google AdMob: Used to serve ads. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA). Personalized ads require App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission at launch.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app purchases. Only purchase verification is performed; no personal information is collected.

3. Advertising

The App displays ads to free-plan users via Google AdMob. Your device's advertising ID may be used. Non-personalized ads are shown even without ATT permission.

4. Data Deletion

All data is stored only on your device. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all stored data.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated without prior notice. Any changes will be posted on this page.
