FAQ

I can't find a stock by searching

In addition to company name, you can search by the 4-digit ticker code (e.g., 7581). If no results appear, a "Add by ticker code" button will show — use that to add it directly.

Shareholder benefit info won't load automatically

Benefit data is fetched automatically from Yahoo Finance Japan. If it can't be retrieved, tap "Enter manually" to input the benefit details and expiration date yourself.

Stock prices aren't updating

Tap the refresh button (↺) in the top-left corner of the home screen to update all stocks at once. You can also update individual stocks from the stock detail screen. Note that Yahoo Finance API data does not update outside of market hours.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored only on your iPhone. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud. Please note that uninstalling the app will permanently delete all data.

What is the Premium plan?

The free plan limits you to 5 registered stocks. Upgrading to Premium removes this limit and hides all ads. Purchase from the Settings screen.

My widget isn't updating

Widgets update automatically when benefit data changes and on a periodic schedule. To force a refresh immediately, long-press the widget on your home screen and tap "Edit Widget."
